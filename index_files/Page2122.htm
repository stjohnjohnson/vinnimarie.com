Poet/Scholar

by Vinni Marie D'Ambrosio, in Bitterroot

A Grand Tour

 

 

He murmured,

Love with you is seeing a

fiery sunrise on the

blue,

blue,

blue

Mediterranean.

She was breathing the

shallows among dark planets,

but thought I'll join him

in scenery-gazing.

So she floated to Europe.

And there beyond the bathing shore,

there along combustion's blue edge,

there curried by the orient light,

a ship slid

like a slow pearl swan,

and she loved it to swooning,

and again lost his sky and his sea.
