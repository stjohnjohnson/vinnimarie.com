Rubaiyat

This is a colorfully narrative poem by New York poet Vinnie-Marie D'Ambrosio lavishly illustrated with complex and playful woodcut prints by Karen Kunc. Inspired by the real home of painters Diego Rivera and Frida Kahlo, this tale creates a rebirth of life through the grand feast day of death, el Di de los Muertos. The book visually creates a life-size calavera for vertical display. 1993. Blue Heron Press.

Mexican Gothic

Dr. D'Ambrosio's poem "Copper Beech" is engraved on the Rothenberg Memorial Sculpture, artist: Madeline Segall-Marx. This work is a wall made up of 49 glass boxes, each featuring a mathematical concept and one featuring Vinni Marie D'Ambrosio's poem. New York City, 1997.
